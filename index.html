Unbeatable Location, Homely Apartments

Larkfield Apartments offers four wonderful properties ideally situated in the centre of Richmond, perfect if you want to leave the car at home.

A 15-minute walk will bring you to Richmond Park, a beautiful stretch of the Thames Riverside, two wonderful theatres and an array of bars, restaurants and shops. Guests can take advantage of the close proximity of the station and high-street, and the ease of access to and from central London.

For those who want to venture further afield, Richmond offers superb transport links to the surrounding areas of Twickenham, Wimbledon, Hampton, Kensington, and even a direct link to Windsor. Whether your visit is for pleasure, business or research, Larkfield Apartments offer the perfect location for your visit to Richmond.

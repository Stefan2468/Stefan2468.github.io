Precautions during Coronavirus Pandemic


During this unforeseen time, we have adapted our practices to fall in line with the UK Government guidelines, surrounding property cleanliness and social distancing. All this is with the aim to make your stay with us as safe as possible.

Our self-catering apartments offer a protected environment, with our guests being the only occupants of the property.

Guidelines we are following :

  • Between bookings, prior to new guests’ arriving, we are ensuring the property has remained dormant for a minimum period of 48 hours.
  • Cleaning practices have been upgraded. The apartment has been sanitised prior to arrival, with particular focus given to high-volume touchpoints (door handles, controls/light switches, TV remotes and taps). All cleaning is carried out using appropriate personal protective equipment.
  • To avoid contamination, no one else will enter the property during your stay.
  • Touchpoints in common areas (e.g. entrance door and staircase handrail) will be sanitised on a daily basis during your stay [First Floor/Studio only].

Guidelines we please ask you to follow :

  • Please maintain social distancing practices and adopt personal protective measures, when applicable, involving protective gear and the regular washing of hands (especially after catching public transport).
  • Please consider periodically cleaning touchpoints within the property during your stay, for example door handles, switches and taps. Cleaning equipment has been provided for you.
  • Please self-monitor for virus symptoms, both in the weeks prior to your stay and during your stay with us.

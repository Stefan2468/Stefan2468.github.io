The One Bedroom Apartment

19 Larkfield Road, Richmond, Surrey TW9 2PG

£100 to £125 per day

Sleeps up to 3. A fully equipped apartment occupying the first floor of a Victorian house in a most convenient part of Richmond. Two minutes walk from shops and station, yet quietly situated away from the main road.

Apartment Layout

Consists of 4 separate rooms:

  • Spacious living room with dining area and two 2 seater lounges.
  • Large bedroom with a double and a single bed. Area for cot if required. Built in wardrobe.
  • Kitchen
  • Bathroom/toilet. Full-size bath with shower.

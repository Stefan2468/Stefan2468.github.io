The Two Bedroom House

5 Cambridge Road, East Twickenham, Middlesex TW1 2HN

£130 to £160 per day

Sleeps up to 4. A fully equipped welcoming house at the foot of Richmond Bridge. Two minutes walk to River Thames, tennis courts, buses and English Heritage parks. Richmond Town Centre is just across the bridge and the underground/main line station is a 15 minute walk away or 5 minutes on one of the many buses. Off-street parking is provided.

Apartment Layout

  • Ground Floor: Spacious open plan lounge/dining area; kitchen; toilet; study; stairs to first floor.
  • First floor: Two double bedrooms each with built-in wardrobes; bathroom/toilet.
  • Outside: Paved patio garden to front and rear; off-street parking bay.
